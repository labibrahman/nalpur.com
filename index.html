nalpur

Nalpur uses a B2B platform to connect apparel brands with manufacturers.

The Nalpur Platform comprises three things:

  1. An aggregated list of fully vetted factories with available capacity and price estimates for manufacturing clothing.
  2. Order management software that simplifies the process of creating samples, sending feedback, and placing a purchase order.
  3. Supply chain management software that tracks the shipment of the product and handles the transfer of funds to the manufacturer.

We haven’t built the platform yet. Before we commit to building it, we’re fulfilling orders the old-fashioned way — over phone and email — so that we can better automate the process when we do build it.

Nalpur is cheaper than existing suppliers because we are working with factories in Bangladesh that are more cost effective than ones based in China, Vietnam or India.

To start working with Nalpur, all you need is a tech pack with your product specifications and target price. We will release this information only to factories that meet both your requirements and international labor standards. Once you agree to a price and an order quantity, we will guide you through the manufacturing process till your shipment arrives in the US.
